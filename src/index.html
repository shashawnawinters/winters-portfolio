A little about me...

Who am I ? Shashawna Winters, Web Developer, Designer, and recent grad from General Assembly Web Development Immersive Program. Merging my love for all things design, I look to continue to build on my current knowledege by continuing to learn and apply that knowledge to new and exciting websites. Before General Assembly I had experience working in the Retail/Fashion and Customer Service Industry, and also had various experience managing my father's companies. I was inspired to get into web design after having websites made for my father and not being satisfied. I decided that I wanted to take the design knowledge I already had and apply it to something new and what better than websites, a computer's blank canvas. I was also inspired by my fashion blog on tumblr and my wix website for my clothing collection I create in my spare time, and the lack of customization available using their templates. That is where the journey began and I am excited to see where my new career takes me!
